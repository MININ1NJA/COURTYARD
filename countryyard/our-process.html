OUR Process

GOATS AND GRAZING

Our goat dairy farms spread around the Aravalli hills in Alwar, Rajasthan.

GOATS AND GRAZING

Our goats are grazed in open fields with green pastures and mountains by shepherds and enjoy eating trees, shrubs, farm grown hay and grains. Our goats are mainly of Jakhrana and Bateesi breed, native to this region.

MILKING PROCESS

The milking process is done in hygienic manner through traditional methods

MILKING PROCESS

The milking process is done in hygienic manner through traditional methods after the kids are fed with the required milk. Our milk is 100% Natural and free from any artificial additives including preservatives and has no added hormones.

CHILLING AND HANDLING

Our fresh goat milk is chilled at farm level and transported to our production.

CHILLING AND HANDLING

Our fresh goat milk is chilled at farm level and transported to our production and packaging area in a hygienic and chilled condition. This improves the quality and protects the product, so it stays fresher for longer.

TESTING & QUALITY CHECKS

Our products are independently tested at various stages.

TESTING & QUALITY CHECKS

Our products are independently tested at various stages and we have strict rules and regulations in place to ensure that our products are of the highest quality.

PACKAGING & DISTRIBUTION

Our products are distributed to our consumers all over India using supply chain.

PACKAGING & DISTRIBUTION

We pack our fresh goat milk in sterilised glass bottles and process a portion of milk for Goat Cheese, Feta, Cajeta, Yogurts and many other healthy products with high nutritional values.
