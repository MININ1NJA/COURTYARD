ABOUT

Courtyard Farms started its journey a few years ago with the mission of education and awareness of the treasures of the rural lifestyle and food habits. Realizing the effect of fortified western products in the market only resolved their need to promote the natural treasures of rural lifestyle to the urban population who always felt the need of such products but could not get it. With their roots and beliefs firmly in agricultural activities, the Courtyard Farms family aims to provide farm fresh produce such as goat milk and cheese from their free-range goats, a doctor recommended substitute for cow milk.

Being a part and contributor of urbanism made them acknowledge and address the lack of accessibility of the healthier options and hence have taken it upon themselves the entire Farm to Table process. They closely oversee the entire farm production including goat breeding, milking, chilling of the milk, pasteurization, packaging, and delivery of their dairy products straight to your table. Their tight-knit family monitors each process with the utmost hygiene to obtain the most optimized quality of milk in an extremely traditional and authentic manner.
